Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for DotEightInc Services?

Paying for DotEightInc services and products is 100% easy and stress free. Choose from your DotAccount dashboard, the product or service you are paying for and click on the button beneath the service or product card to pay using your ATM Card or using USSD.

Kindly note that you will be charged 1.4% processing fee per transactions from the payment gateway.

What`s your refund policy?

We refund back your money if your request for a refund is within the range of the stipulated time indicated in every services.

On refund, we give back 95% of your initial pay because of charges encountered during refund.

Please note that, we do not offer refund for payment on use of products. We only offer refunds for active and delivered services.

What`s a Domain Name?

A domain name is a unique identity that recognizes you on the web. Once you register a domain name, no other person can use that domain untill it expires. A domain name is always renewed yearly. A domain name comes with different domain extensions like .com, .com.ng, .ng, .xyz, .org e.t.c

Presently, DotEightInc offers free a .com.ng domain when you buy an annual hosting plan with us

Note that we do not refund fee for domain registrations.

Can I build my website myself and Host with DotEightInc?

Sure! Why not?

DotEightInc has a service for web developers which enables you to launch your website on our hosting plans. We also give you a free .com.ng domain when you purchase an annual hosting plan with us.

Can I Upload my Android Application on DotEightInc Playstore Console?

Yes! You can.

We give mobile developers the right to publish their application on our Playstore console for a fee of NGN3 000 per year.

Before we publish your app, your app undergoes 1 week of verification to check out what your application is really about and doesn`t contain any illegal activities

We also give you back a refund after 1 week of publishing your app, if you aren`t satisfied with our service

How do I delete or deactivate my DotAccount?

Incase you feel unsecured, you can suspend your account anytime from the account settings tab on your DotAccount dashboard
